About Us

Student Education - We help children to unlock their potential and realize their dreams. We believe that every child is extraordinary and can scale great heights if provided with the right learning opportunities. With regular and generous contributions from our supporters, we are able to march steadily towards achieving these goals.

We are committed to ensuring that all children, irrespective of their origin, are able to go to school, play, interact and learn with other children of their age. We help children with limited means to hone their creativity and skills.

Our Mission

Educating and empowering the less privileged through integrated methods and initiating and implementing progressive social change.We want to see an India where every child gets an education, every youth an opportunity to succeed, and every woman the right to equality.

Our Visions

KASH Educational Trust is dedicated to support the weaker sections of the society with a holistic, sustainable and diversified approach. Our vision is socio-economic and educational development of backward communities students, with a focus on child development and youth development.

-- Our Story --

KASH Educational Trust was established in 2013 with the aim of providing quality education to children of poor background. The school provides quality education to children from socially, economically and educationally weaker section of the society.

KASH plays an important role in bringing a positive change in the lives of these children by isolating them from the present situation and providing them a healthy environment to sustain and at the same time providing them education to become self reliant in future.
